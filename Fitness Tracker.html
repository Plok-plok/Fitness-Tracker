Men's Go-To Workouts

Push-Ups

Description: A great upper body workout targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

1. Get into a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.

2. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground.

3. Push back up to the starting position.

Squats

Description: A lower body exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.

2. Lower your hips as if sitting in a chair.

3. Return to standing position.

Plank

Description: A core stability exercise that strengthens the abs, back, and shoulders.

1. Get into a push-up position but rest on your forearms.

2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

3. Hold the position as long as possible.
